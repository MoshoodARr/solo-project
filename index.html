Ile Ife

National Museum Ile Ife is one of the well-known tourist destinations in Ile Ife. The Ooni of Ife Palace is next to the museum. The National Commission for Museums and Monuments is in charge of its own on behalf of the federal government.

Ibodi Monkey Reserve

Ibodi Monkey Reserve is a monkey forest located in the Atakumosa West Local Government Area of Osun State, Nigeria.
